# regulations

Country based laws and regulations that may be critical considered for doing business with and planning technical architecture.

Working as enterprise architect in financial industry, we're facing more and more laws & regulations that need to be considered when deal with variable markets or target customer groups.

This repository aims to collect as much as possible of the relevant laws (acts) and regulations (also rules) from information technology, financing & insurance industray, government general interests and cross-border constraints, etc...

Gradually, the sub-folders will be created once there're group of specific domains are relevant within certain markets.
